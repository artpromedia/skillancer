## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Visit [skillancer.com](https://skillancer.com) and click **"Sign Up as Freelancer"**
2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
3. Accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
4. Click **"Create Account"**

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

1. Check your inbox for a verification email from Skillancer
2. Click the verification link within 24 hours
3. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder or request a new one

### Step 3: Complete Initial Setup

1. Choose your primary skill category
2. Select your experience level
3. Set your timezone and preferred language
4. Enable two-factor authentication (recommended)

## Setting Up Your Profile

Your profile is your storefront—make it count! Complete profiles get **3x more job invitations**.

### Essential Profile Elements

#### Profile Photo

- Use a professional headshot
- Ensure good lighting and a neutral background
- Face should be clearly visible
- Minimum resolution: 400x400 pixels

#### Professional Headline

Write a compelling headline that showcases your expertise:

**Good Examples:**

- "Senior Full-Stack Developer | React & Node.js Expert | 8+ Years Experience"
- "UI/UX Designer Specializing in SaaS Products | Figma & Adobe XD"
- "DevOps Engineer | AWS Certified | Kubernetes & Terraform"

#### Overview/Bio

- Start with your strongest selling point
- Highlight relevant experience and achievements
- Include specific technologies and methodologies
- Keep it between 200-400 words
- Use bullet points for readability

#### Hourly Rate

- Research market rates for your skills and experience
- Consider starting slightly lower to build reviews
- You can adjust your rate as you gain platform reputation

### Portfolio Items

Add 3-5 of your best work samples:

1. Click **"Add Portfolio Item"**
2. Upload images, videos, or links
3. Write a brief description of the project
4. Highlight your specific contributions
5. Include technologies used

### Employment History

Add relevant work experience:

- Company name and your title
- Duration of employment
- Key responsibilities and achievements
- This helps establish credibility

## Verifying Your Skills

Skill verification increases your visibility and builds trust with clients.

### Verification Methods

#### 1. Skills Assessments

- Take platform-provided skill tests
- Tests are timed and proctored
- Passing scores earn verified badges
- Retake available after 30 days

#### 2. SkillPod Verification

- Complete tasks in a secure, monitored environment
- Real-world skill demonstration
- Higher trust score than traditional tests
- Session recordings available for clients

#### 3. External Credentials

- Link GitHub profile for developers
- Connect LinkedIn for work history
- Upload certifications (AWS, Google Cloud, etc.)
- Verify degrees and diplomas

### Verification Levels

| Level         | Requirements                 | Benefits                           |
| ------------- | ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **Basic**     | Complete profile             | Access to all jobs                 |
| **Verified**  | 1+ skill verification        | Verified badge, priority in search |
| **Expert**    | 3+ verifications + reviews   | Expert badge, top search placement |
| **Top Rated** | High earnings + satisfaction | Premium support, exclusive jobs    |

## Finding Your First Job

### Using Job Search

1. Navigate to **"Find Work"** from the main menu
2. Use filters to narrow results:
   - **Category**: Your expertise area
   - **Budget**: Minimum/maximum range
   - **Duration**: Project length
   - **Experience Level**: Entry, Intermediate, Expert
   - **Client History**: Verified, has hire history

### Job Feed Personalization

- The platform learns your preferences over time
- Save searches for quick access
- Enable notifications for matching jobs
- Use **"Best Matches"** tab for AI-recommended jobs

### Understanding Job Posts

Key elements to evaluate:

- **Client verification status**: Look for verified clients
- **Client history**: Jobs posted, hire rate, total spent
- **Job description**: Clear scope and requirements
- **Budget**: Fixed-price or hourly
- **Timeline**: Realistic expectations
- **Required skills**: Match your expertise

### Red Flags to Avoid

⚠️ Be cautious of:

- Requests for free work samples
- Asking to communicate off-platform
- Unrealistic expectations for budget
- Vague project descriptions
- Requests for personal financial information

## Submitting Winning Proposals

### Proposal Structure

#### 1. Personalized Opening

- Reference specific details from the job post
- Show you've read and understood the requirements
- Avoid generic templates

#### 2. Demonstrate Expertise

- Highlight relevant experience
- Link to similar portfolio items
- Mention specific technologies/methodologies

#### 3. Proposed Approach

- Outline how you would tackle the project
- Include estimated timeline
- Suggest potential improvements or alternatives

#### 4. Call to Action

- Express enthusiasm for the project
- Invite further discussion
- Ask a thoughtful question to start dialogue

### Proposal Best Practices

✅ **Do:**

- Respond within 24 hours of job posting
- Keep it concise (150-300 words)
- Proofread for errors
- Include relevant portfolio links
- Be realistic about timeline and budget

❌ **Don't:**

- Copy-paste generic templates
- Lowball just to win the job
- Promise what you can't deliver
- Share personal contact information
- Apply to jobs outside your expertise

### Connect Costs

- Each proposal costs 2-4 Connects depending on job value
- New accounts receive free Connects to start
- Connects can be purchased in bundles
- Unused Connects roll over monthly

## Getting Paid

### Payment Protection

Skillancer protects your earnings through:

- **Fixed-Price**: Milestone-based escrow
- **Hourly**: Weekly payment cycles with work diary

### Setting Up Payments

1. Go to **Settings → Payment Methods**
2. Add your preferred withdrawal method:
   - Bank transfer (ACH/Wire)
   - PayPal
   - Payoneer
   - Wise
3. Complete identity verification
4. Set your payment schedule (weekly/bi-weekly)

### Payment Timeline

| Event                 | Timeline             |
| --------------------- | -------------------- |
| Milestone approved    | Available in 5 days  |
| Hourly work week ends | Available in 10 days |
| Withdrawal requested  | 1-3 business days    |

### Service Fees

Skillancer charges a sliding service fee:

| Lifetime Billings with Client | Fee |
| ----------------------------- | --- |
| First $500                    | 20% |
| $500.01 - $10,000             | 10% |
| Over $10,000                  | 5%  |

### Managing Finances

- Track earnings in the **Cockpit Dashboard**
- Download invoices for tax purposes
- View detailed transaction history
- Set up automatic withdrawals
